About Me

I am a marine biologist and molecular ecologist based at the University of the Ryukyus, Okinawa. My research focuses on hard corals (Scleractinia), exploring their taxonomy, diversity, physiological performance, and genetic adaptation in response to climate change and human disturbances.

Research Interests

  • Hard coral taxonomy and species diversity
  • Community dynamics under environmental change
  • Physiological performance at high-latitude and mesophotic depths
  • Population genetics and genomic adaptation
  • Data analysis and visualization in R
